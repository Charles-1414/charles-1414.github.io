Game Guide


There are 3 colors of bricks, referring 3 levels of hardness. The order from the weakest to the hardest is: Green, Yellow, Purple


You have 3 lives. When the ball hit the ground, it will revive if the lives haven't been used up and the remaining life will decrease.

Each time you hit a brick, you will get 10 points. Each remaining life worth 100 points. And each remaining second worth 15 points.


You have some skills: Speed UP and Fire.

The ball's speed will double if speed up skill is enabled.

The ball will cut through the bricks if fire skill is enabled.

Each skill will last for 3 seconds.

BTW, press 1 to enable Speed UP and press 2 to enable Fire.
